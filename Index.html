Campaign Breakdown: The New Series
1. Monday Mix (Rotating Topics) The Vibe: Fresh start to the week. Rotation Ideas:
Week 1: Society News (Meeting reminders, Zoom links).
Week 2: Member Bloom Room (Sharing a photo of what’s blooming in a member's house).
Week 3: Volunteer Spotlight (Thanking someone "behind the scenes").
Week 4: Prep for the Show (Countdown/Logistics).
2. Troubleshoot Tuesday (The "Clinic") The Focus: Visual identification of problems. Content Ideas:
"Is it Sunburn or Fungus?" (Side-by-side photo guides).
"The Pest Patrol" (Identifying mealybugs, scale, or spider mites).
"Potting Panic" (When to repot and how to tell if the media is "sour").
Call To Action: "Post a photo of your 'problem plant' in the comments for a community check-up!" 3. Wednesday's Genus/Species Gallery (Botanical Education) The Focus: High-level overviews of specific orchid genera. The Content: Discussing the history, general care, and unique traits of a genus (e.g., Cattleya or Dendrobium) without getting lost in specific species names yet. Call To Action: "Do you have a favorite in this genus? Tell us why!" 4. Thirsty Thursday (Culture & Maintenance) The Focus: The 5-week rotation for keeping plants alive and thriving. Rotation:
Week 1: Method (Soaker vs. Sprayer).
Week 2: Water Quality (Rain vs. Tap).
Week 3: Environment (Humidity & Air).
Week 4: Signs of Thirst (Reading Pseudobulbs).
Week 5: The Deep Clean (Salt Flushing).
5. Fun Fact Friday (The "Historian" Corner) The Focus: Weird, wild, and historical orchid trivia. Topics: Vanilla (the edible orchid), Victorian "Orchid Hunters," mimicry (orchids that look like animals), and extreme longevity. Call To Action: "Did you know this? Share your favorite plant fact below!"
